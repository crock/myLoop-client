The Christian Chamber



Story

It all started when our chamber came to a pivotal point just 5 years ago. We lost sight of our true purpose, with no genuine engagement happening and no true relationships being built. Who was going to step up to the plate to help this community get to its inner self? Mark Goldstein.

He revived the chamber, getting members to connect with the real why, allowing this community to blossom into the group it is today. The Christian Chamber takes every chance it gets to not only impact each other, but empower others to do the same.
